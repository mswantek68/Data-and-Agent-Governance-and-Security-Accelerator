Why Now

Users are prompting AI today; security and governance controls need to catch up to protect production agent workloads.

The Governance Gap

AI interactions across Copilot, Foundry agents, and Fabric need consistent security controls. Without DSPM and Defender, prompts bypass data loss prevention, diagnostic settings become inconsistent, and auditors lack visibility.

Challenges

Copilot and browser AI prompts bypass traditional DLP rules unless DSPM for AI captures and stores them in Exchange Online.

Foundry projects become inconsistent when diagnostics, Defender plans, and Content Safety settings are configured manually.

Audit, eDiscovery, and security operations teams need retained prompts, scan results, and policy evidence in a central location.

Paths this accelerator covers

M365 Copilot & 3rd-party AI: DSPM for AI policies capture prompts/responses to mailboxes for retention, eDiscovery, and DLP.

Microsoft Foundry & custom agents: Defender for AI threat detection + diagnostics to Log Analytics; Purview data-source classification; Content Safety blocklists.

Run tags you need: m365 (Copilot path), dspm (scans/audit), defender (plans), foundry (registration & safety).

Technical proof points

A single configuration file drives ordered modules that enable Purview DSPM for AI, Defender for AI, Content Safety, Azure Policy assignments, and exportable evidence.

Configuration file: A single JSON file (spec.local.json) defines tenants, Purview accounts, Foundry projects, policies, and export settings.

Orchestrator: Tag-based execution (foundation, m365, dspm, defender, foundry) runs modules in the correct order.

Controls enabled: DSPM scans and Secure Interactions, DLP policies with sensitivity labels and retention, Defender for AI with diagnostics, Content Safety blocklists, Azure Policy assignments.

Evidence: Compliance inventory, audit log exports, and diagnostics sent to Log Analytics for security operations—ready for audits and change requests.

How it works

From configuration to governed AI environment in four steps.

1) Define

Copy spec.dspm.template.json → spec.local.json; add tenant, subscriptions, Purview accounts, Foundry projects, DLP/labels/retention, and audit export settings.

2) Deploy

Run azd up or run.ps1 with your chosen tags. Policies, data sources, scans, and Defender plans are applied automatically.

3) Verify

Purview Audit captures Foundry AI activity. DSPM for AI stores Copilot prompts in Exchange Online for retention and eDiscovery. Content Safety blocklists protect agents.

4) Evidence

Export audit logs and policy snapshots locally—ready for security reviews and compliance audits.
